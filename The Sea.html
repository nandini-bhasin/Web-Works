The Sea

The sea can be angry.
The sea can be rough.
The sea can be wild.
The sea can be tough.

The sea can be rip.
The sea can be tear.
The sea can be roar,
Like a hungry bear.

The sea can be gentle.
The sea can be flat.
The sea can be calm,
As a sleeping cat.

The sea can be glide
Over the sand,
Stroking the beach
Like a giant hand
